Courses

This page displays a list of all possible courses offered. Clicking the "Enroll" button next to any course will enroll you for that course, and clicking the "Drop" button will allow you to drop the course (withdraw your enrollment).

The magnifying glass will allow you to see the reports you have submitted to any course you are currently enrolled in, and the green diamond (a stamp icon) will allow you to send e-mails to the professor or others currently enrolled in that class.
