Welcome to COMTOR

If this is your first time logging on to the website there are some important steps that you need to do in order to continue. First you will need to enroll in your class in order to access the Dropbox, submit code and view instructor assignments on this website. Next you must select the class that you want to submit code to. It is also recommended that you take the time to change your password from the temporary one sent to your inbox.

Signing up

To sign up for your class click on Courses under Course Management. Please choose the appropriate course and class section and click on the Enroll button.

Selecting a Class

Once you have enrolled in your selected classes you will then choose which class you want to view at the Welcome screen and the Dropbox button will become available where you will submit assignments.

Change Password

To change your password click on Account Management and you will see the appropriate link above your name.
