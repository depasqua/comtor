Email

On this page, you may e-mail the professor teaching your course, as well as your fellow classmates. To create an e-mail, simply select the recipients from the drop-down menu under "Recipients" (names are listed in alphabetical order by first name). Multiple names can be selected by clicking multiple names while holding the "Control" button on a PC (the "Command" button on a Mac). You may also highlight all names in a range between two names by clicking on those names while holding the "Shift" button.

Once you have selected the recipients, simply type in a subject in the text box under "Subject", then compose your e-mail in the text box under "E-Mail".
