Requests

On this page, there are a few different requests you can make. The first is changing your account type. To do so, choose an "Account Type" from the drop-down menu under "Account Type," state your reason for the change in the text box, and then click "Request Account Type Change".

You can also request account removal. To do so, simply click the "Request Account Removal" link. You may also cancel any requests you make by simply clicking "Delete Request" next to any of them.
